Driving Climate Impact and Capital Growth: A 10-Month Digital Transformation for a Global Green Economy Pioneer in Africa

CLIENT:

EcoShift Capital

DURATION:

11 Months(September 2024 - Present)

ROLE:

Web Development and Brand Manager

INDUSTRY:

Climate Tech & Sustainable Economic Development

PROJECT OVERVIEW

We partnered with EcoShift Capital, an innovative organization committed to transforming the global challenge of climate change into a catalyst for economic growth. Their mission: harness sustainability projects, green technology, and renewable energy investments to create both environmental impact and business opportunities.

Our role was to design a dynamic, purpose-driven website, develop a cohesive digital strategy, and implement marketing initiatives to position the company as a thought leader in the climate economy space.

OBJECTIVES

  • Build a modern, high-impact website that communicates both environmental and economic value.
  • Increase global visibility among stakeholders, investors, and sustainability advocates.
  • Drive thought leadership through content marketing, research dissemination, and storytelling.
  • Establish a consistent brand identity across all digital platforms.
  • Create digital engagement pathways for partnerships, collaborations, and funding opportunities.

The Challenges

  • No central digital hub to communicate the brand’s vision, projects, and results.
  • Difficulty translating complex climate-economic concepts into clear, relatable messaging for diverse audiences.
  • Low online visibility, making it hard to attract international investors and collaborators.
  • No structured digital outreach plan to connect with key climate action networks and policy influencers.

Our Strategy

Our Strategy was divided in 5 phases: Phase Alpha, Phase Bravo, Phase Charlie, Phase Delta, Phase Eco.
We are currently at Phase Delta

Phase Alpha

  • Reviewed the client’s brief and objectives, aligning them with both brand positioning and sustainability impact goals.
  • Conducted a holistic market and audience analysis, focusing on climate economy stakeholders, investors, policy makers, and the public.
  • Created a custom strategy and digital roadmap tailored to position the company as a leader in climate-driven economic innovation.

Phase Bravo

  • Broke the master strategy into focused execution components: web development, brand positioning, digital marketing, and stakeholder engagement.
  • Set clear timelines and measurable deliverables for each workstream.
  • Aggressively executed, tracked, and synchronized activities across all channels to maintain message consistency and momentum.

Website Design & Development

  • Designed a fully responsive, mobile-first website with intuitive navigation and impactful storytelling.
  • Integrated online ordering and prescription refill system.
  • Added product categories, health resources, and FAQs to boost usability.

Digital Strategy Development

  • Conducted SEO keyword research to target high-intent pharmacy-related searches.
  • Developed a content marketing plan featuring blogs, industry insights, case studies, and multimedia storytelling..
  • Created a social media content calendar to engage policy makers, investors, and the global sustainability community.
  • Implemented LinkedIn thought-leadership campaigns and Twitter (X) policy engagement threads to boost influence.
  • Tracked performance via Google Analytics, refining strategy based on results.

Phase Charlie

  • Evaluated performance using measurable KPIs: website traffic, investor inquiries, social engagement, and keyword rankings.
  • Audited each strategy component to determine what worked vs. what didn’t.
  • Extracted actionable insights to sharpen future content, campaigns, and stakeholder outreach.

Phase Delta(current)

  • Doubled down on top-performing strategies identified in the Charlie phase — particularly high-impact thought-leadership and investor engagement content.
  • Reinforced brand consistency across all channels to strengthen recognition in the climate economy sector.
